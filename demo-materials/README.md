## üé¨ Quick Start Guide

### For a 5-Minute Demo:

1. **Read the Script** (15 minutes)
   - Open VIDEO_DEMO_SCRIPT.md
   - Familiarize yourself with all 11 scenes
   - Practice your narration

2. **Set Up Test Emails** (10 minutes)
   - Open SAMPLE_EMAILS.md
   - Create 4-6 sample emails in your test Outlook account
   - Mark them as unread

3. **Configure Recording** (15 minutes)
   - Follow RECORDING_SETUP_GUIDE.md
   - Install recording software (OBS Studio recommended)
   - Test audio and video quality
   - Clean your desktop

4. **Record** (30-45 minutes)
   - Follow the script scene by scene
   - Don't worry about small mistakes
   - Take breaks between sections

5. **Edit** (1-2 hours)
   - Cut out mistakes and dead space
   - Add captions and annotations
   - Include title card and end screen
   - Export in MP4 format

**Total Time Investment:** 3-4 hours for complete demo

## üé§ Audio Tips

### For Best Audio Quality:

1. **Use a microphone** (not laptop built-in)
   - USB microphone: $80-130
   - Or quality headset: $50-100

2. **Record in quiet space**
   - Close windows
   - Turn off AC/fans
   - Silence notifications

3. **Test before full recording**
   - Record 30 seconds
   - Play back to check
   - Adjust levels as needed

4. **Speak clearly**
   - Not too fast
   - Enunciate words
   - Smile while speaking (sounds friendlier!)
